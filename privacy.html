Privacy Policy – Prizer

Prizer respects user privacy. We collect mobile numbers solely for the purpose of user authentication using SMS-based One-Time Passwords (OTP).

Information Collected

OTPs are sent only after user consent and are used strictly for login and transaction verification. Prizer does not send promotional messages.
